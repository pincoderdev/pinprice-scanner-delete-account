Delete Your Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly inside the app. No email or form needed.

How to delete your account

  1. Open APP_NAME_PLACEHOLDER on your device.
  2. Tap the Account tab (bottom navigation).
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the prompt. Your account and personal data will be deleted immediately.
What gets deleted: your display name, email address, phone number, and location history are permanently removed. Non-personal data such as product names and price contributions are retained in anonymised form to keep the shared product catalogue accurate.

Can't access the app? Email us at support@pinprice.app with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and we will process your request within 30 days.
